How It Works

Three steps. Thirty seconds.

1

Open the Bot

Tap the button below or search @StreamTrackerBot in Telegram and hit /start.

2

3

Get Notified

Commands

The full command reference

Everything the bot can do, at a glance.

/start
Register with the bot and see the welcome message with all available commands.
/track <show>
Search for a TV show and add it to your tracking list. You'll get notified when new episodes air.
/track The Bear
/untrack <show>
Remove a show from your tracking list. Uses fuzzy matching, so you don't need the exact name.
/untrack bear
/find <show>
See every streaming platform a show is available on, with direct links to watch. Great for deciding where to sign up.
/find Breaking Bad
/list
See all the shows you're currently tracking, with status indicators and next episode info.
/next
View all upcoming episodes across your tracked shows for the next 7 days, grouped by date.
/settings
Change your notification time (6 AM – 9 AM) and timezone (Eastern, Central, Mountain, Pacific).
/help
Show the full help message with command descriptions and examples.
FAQ

Questions? We got you.

Is StreamTracker really free? ▼
Yep, 100% free. No premium tier, no hidden fees, no ads. Just open it in Telegram and start tracking.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
Nope. Your Telegram account is your account. Just open the bot and hit /start — that's it. No email, no password, no forms.
What streaming platforms does it support? ▼
StreamTracker checks across 25+ platforms including Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, Max, Prime Video, Paramount+, Apple TV+, Peacock, AMC+, Starz, Crunchyroll, and many more. If a show is streaming somewhere in the US, we'll probably find it.
When do notifications get sent? ▼
By default, you'll get a notification at 9 AM Eastern on the day an episode airs. You can change the time and timezone with the /settings command.
How many shows can I track? ▼
As many as you want. There's no limit. Go wild.
Can my friends use it too? ▼
Absolutely! Just send them the link to @StreamTrackerBot. Everyone gets their own separate watchlist, settings, and notifications.
