FAQ

Before You Try It

Is StreamTracker in beta? ▼
Yes. StreamTracker is still a small beta while the tracking, notification, and suggestion flows get tightened up.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
Nope. Your Telegram chat is enough. Open the bot and start from the buttons or command menu.
What streaming platforms does it support? ▼
StreamTracker checks across 25+ platforms including Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, Max, Prime Video, Paramount+, Apple TV+, Peacock, AMC+, Starz, Crunchyroll, and many more. If a show is streaming somewhere in the US, we'll probably find it.
When do notifications get sent? ▼
By default, reminders are set for 9 AM Eastern. You can change the time and timezone with /settings.
How many shows can I track? ▼
There is no hard beta limit right now, though the product is still being tuned for wider usage.
What data does it store? ▼
Your Telegram chat ID, tracked shows, reminder time, timezone, and basic activity timestamps. It does not ask for streaming passwords, payment details, email, or phone number.
